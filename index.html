Organisation des Services Libres d'Internet

The OSLI provides access to various media and Internet services for everyone, free, forever. The organisation is founded upon the principle that everyone, regardless of background, should be able to access media and communicate easily and freely on the Internet.

Currently, due to limited funding, the number of services offered by the organisation is small. However, we do provide:

  • RadioFOD: A free indie rock radio (link)

Check this space, as more features will be provided as time goes on. Currently, we are in the process of setting up an IRC server.
